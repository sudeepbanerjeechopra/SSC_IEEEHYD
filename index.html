Section Student Congress (SSC) provides a platform for all Student Branches to come together, share thoughts, ideas, and best practices, and learn from each other. It aims to improve the overall functioning of branches while giving students the opportunity to interact with peers from various colleges, fostering common goals in alignment with IEEE’s vision. This convention also offers budding entrepreneurs a chance to present their ideas, providing a solid foundation for their career growth. Various technical and non-technical events are planned during the Congress.

  • This year's Section Student Congress, focuses on the theme of "Semiconductors: Shaping the Future."

Semiconductors are the backbone of modern technology, powering everything from smartphones to complex computer systems. They play a pivotal role in enabling innovations across industries like healthcare, communications, and renewable energy.

The focus on semiconductors aligns with the growing importance of this technology in driving future advancements and innovations. With the rise of AI, IoT, and 5G, semiconductors are at the heart of enabling new possibilities, pushing boundaries in computing, and enhancing connectivity worldwide. This theme explores how advancements in semiconductor technology are contributing to a smarter, more connected world, and how students can be part of this transformative journey. Through this Congress, participants will delve into the technical aspects of semiconductor innovation, industry trends, and their applications, learning how semiconductors will shape the future of technology and society.
